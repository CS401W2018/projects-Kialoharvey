Hero Page - Denzel Washington

Interests Page | Hero Page

Denzel Washington is one of the most iconic actors of our time. His work spans decades, and he has portrayed many memorable characters, from Malcolm X to Alonzo Harris in *Training Day*. Beyond his on-screen talent, Denzel is a role model for leadership, integrity, and excellence both in Hollywood and in his personal life.
