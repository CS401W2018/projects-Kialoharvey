  • Meet the Parrs—a family of superheroes disguised as ordinary people. Together, they’re an unstoppable team.
  • Bob, Helen, Violet, Dash, and baby Jack-Jack each have their own unique powers.
  • They love to go on adventures and to help save others from dangers!

. Bob Parr, also known as Mr. Incredible, has super strength and is the powerhouse of the Parr family. Even though Bob tries to live a normal life, he’s always ready to jump into action to protect his family and help others. His kindness and strong loyalty makes him a hero people can’t forget, whether he’s in his superhero suit or not.

Helen Parr, also known as Elastigirl, is flexible, and has the ability to stretch her body to incredible lengths. Helen is resourceful and courageous, always ready to tackle any challenge. With the ability to stretch her body to incredible lengths, Her strength, intelligence, and sense of humor make her the true backbone of the Parr family.

Dash Parr, lives up to his name with super speed, making him one of the fastest kids around. Dash is always looking for an adventure and is brave and loyal. He brings fun and excitement to the family and is a hero in his own right.
