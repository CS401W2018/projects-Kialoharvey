Color Selection for Styling

Avatar: The Last Airbender is one of my favorite TV shows because it's a story about four nations that control the elements of earth, fire, air, and water. It's full of epic battles, and profound themes of balance and harmony.

The Fire Nation is the tribe that seeks to control all four nations, but the Avatar, who is the only one capable of bending all four elements, protects the other nations from the Fire Nation's oppression. The show's journey is one of leadership, and the importance of peace.

  • Emphasized text color: I chose light blue to emphasize key elements such as links and highlighted text, as it's attention-grabbing but not overwhelming.
  • De-emphasized text color: I used dark gray for the body text, as it's easy on the eyes and doesn't distract from the main points.
  • Color selection process: I selected these colors because they offer a good balance of contrast and readability. I used a color palette tool online to find complementary colors that fit well together.
