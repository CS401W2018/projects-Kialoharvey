Denzel Washington is a very famous actor known around the world. He has acted in movies for many years and played many unforgettable roles, like Malcolm X and Alonzo Harris in Training Day. Beyond his on-screen talent, Denzel is a role model for leadership, integrity, and excellence both in Hollywood and in his personal life.
