The Energy Sector

This page explores the importance of the Energy sector.

  • Jobs and Growth: The energy industry creates many jobs, like building power plants and using renewable energy sources.
  • National Security: Producing our own energy means we don’t depend on other countries. This keeps the country safe and prevents supply problems
  • Environment: Clean energy, like solar and wind, reduces pollution and helps fight climate change by cutting harmful emissions【
