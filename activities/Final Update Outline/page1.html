Topic: Different Business Sectors

This page explains what types of business sector have a high revenue. Business sectors are divisions of industries or areas within the economy where businesses function, categorized based on their activities and the services they provide.

Comparison of Different business types.
Business Type Description Revenue(%)
Energy Sector The energy sector provides power for homes, businesses, and industries. It includes companies that produce oil, gas, electricity, and renewable energy like solar and wind. 10-12
Technology sector The technology sector focuses on developing tools and systems to make life easier and businesses more efficient. It includes companies that create computers, smartphones, software, and digital platforms. 8-10
Online Retail Sector Online retail is about selling products and services over the internet. Companies like Amazon and Alibaba allow customers to shop from anywhere using their phones or computers 4-5
